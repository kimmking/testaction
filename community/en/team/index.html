Becoming a Committer

The ShardingSphere community follows the Apache Community’s process on accepting a new committer. After a contributor participate ShardingSphere community actively, (P)PMC and Committers will make decision to invite the contributor join Committers and (P)PMC.

You can read Contributor Guide to participate the community, and can get more information on Committer Guide.
